🎮 How 2 Player Mode Works
01

Pick a Mode

Choose 2 Players for same-device battles, or vs AI to play solo against the machine.

02

Set AI Difficulty

Easy AI makes mistakes. Medium AI is decent. Hard AI plays near-perfectly. All 3 available in every game.

03

Share the Keyboard

Most games use split keyboard controls. P1 uses WASD, P2 uses Arrow keys — or just take turns clicking.

04

Mobile Friendly

On mobile, games use on-screen touch controls. Split-screen games show two sets of buttons.
