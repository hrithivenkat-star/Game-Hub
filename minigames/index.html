Snake tip

Hug the walls and spiral inward — this gives you the most room to maneuver as you grow.

🟦

Tetris tip

Keep your stack flat and low. Save the I-piece for clearing 4 rows (Tetris!) at once.

🔢

2048 tip

Pick one corner and always swipe toward it. Keep your highest tile locked in that corner.
